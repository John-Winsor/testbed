Arthur Handley was born Arthur Curle and was NOT a descendant of the Handley family.  His Birth Certificate does not identify his father and leaves only a blank space.  So Arthur's birth was registered in his mother's surname.  Subsequently his mother married Walter John Handley who, in the course of time, gave his surname to his step-son.
